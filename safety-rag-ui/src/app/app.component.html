Working at Heights Safety Assistant

Ask practical questions about working at heights in NSW and get grounded answers from official guidance and checklists.

How to use

  • Ask one clear question at a time.
  • Mention the context, for example roof work, ladders, scaffolds.
  • Use it as a safety reminder, not a legal opinion.
  • When in doubt, check SafeWork NSW or your safety officer.

Example questions

  • What is considered working at heights in NSW?
  • When do I need a fall arrest system instead of a ladder?
  • What PPE is required when using a mobile scaffold?
